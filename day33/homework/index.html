Did You Know?

Discover amazing fun facts about animals, space, history, and more!

Animal Facts

  • Cats sleep for about 70% of their lives.
  • Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood!
  • A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance".
  • Elephants are the only animals that can't jump.

Space Facts

  • A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus.
  • There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth.
  • The largest volcano in the solar system is on Mars, called Olympus Mons.
  • Neutron stars are so dense that a sugar-cube-sized amount would weigh billions of tons!

History Facts

  • The Great Pyramid of Giza was the tallest man-made structure for over 3,800 years.
  • The shortest war in history lasted 38 minutes!
  • Cleopatra lived closer to the invention of the iPhone than to the construction of the Great Pyramid.
